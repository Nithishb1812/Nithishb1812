Your Affordable Housing Lender

Products

Rocky Mountain CRC is a team of professionals ready to help you accomplish your goals by providing you the best product, custom fit to get you to the finish line. Rocky Mountain CRC specializes in perm financing for multifamily rental properties serving low and moderate-income households. Products are crafted to accommodate the use of federal subsidies, including Section 8 HAP, USDA-RD 515 and Section 42 Low-Income Housing Tax Credits. Check out our affordable housing and community development loan products below to find the one that meets your needs. Can’t find it? Call us and let’s innovate!

Investor LIHTC

Permanent loans for multifamily real estate projects during the initial LIHTC compliance period with the LIHTC investor holding majority ownership of the borrowing entity.

LIHTC community projects that are:

  • Up to 40-year amortization
  • Up to 16-year maturity
  • Up to 30-month forward commitment
  • Competitive rates based on Treasury

Non-Investor LIHTC

Permanent loans for multifamily real estate properties during the extended LIHTC compliance period.

LIHTC community properties that are:

  • Seeking acquisition
  • Up to 40-year amortization
  • Up to 10-year maturity
  • Competitive rates based on Treasury

Affordable Non-LIHTC

Permanent loans for affordable housing multifamily real estate projects. Projects that meet our affordability requirements and are:

  • Seeking acquisition
  • Up to 40-year amortization
  • Up to 16-year maturity
  • Competitive rates based on Treasury
